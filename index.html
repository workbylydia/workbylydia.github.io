Research Intake &
Bandwidth Tracking

Cisco Systems · 2022–2024

Centralized visibility for a research team operating in the dark

"There was no single place to track what research projects were in flight, who owned them, or how much bandwidth UX researchers had — requests came in informally with no visibility for leadership."

250%
increase in studies YoY
↓
study turnaround time
↓
cost per study
Key deliverables
Centralized project dashboard Researcher bandwidth tracking Standardized intake playbook Leadership reporting system Researcher onboarding program

How I approached it

  • Started with a Microsoft Form intake — quickly identified it created a new form per request rather than updating one source of truth, and iterated forward
  • Explored MS Power BI to create dashboard reporting for leadership on project status and researcher bandwidth
  • Partnered with DPM to surface bandwidth data that shaped research strategy and managed stakeholder expectations
  • Standardized the intake process and embedded it into onboarding — making it the default, not the exception

Business impact

By centralizing intake and making researcher bandwidth visible, leadership could shape research strategy proactively — and the team went from reactive to systematically scalable. Studies increased 250% YoY while cost per study dropped.
